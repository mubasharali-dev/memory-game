How to play?

Memory Game is basically a mind memory game. Sounds Confusing? Let me explain you more. The first is to take one card and search for that card from the all cards. You have to remember the previous card you clicked. When the card you got previously and current card are same, you'll jump to match the next two cards.

The more cards you remember at one time the more faster you win the game.

This matching game is about to match 2 cards of same images from 24 cards. All the cards are hidden by a same Image card.

Click the card and search for that same second card from entire list. If two cards are not same, those cards will flip back again and the loop will continues.

To know about card names and their visual representation, please visit Cards.

As you can see, all the cards are hidden by one JavaScript Badge. So there is no chance to get the same card at same place everytime. It'll be changed after every new game. You short term mind memory will surely help you to win this game. Thank you!
